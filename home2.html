Hello World!

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Connecticut, double majoring in Computer Science & Engineering and Electrical Engineering. My strong interest in programming, problem solving, and digital systems led my academic career toward a double major, and I am enjoying every bit of it.

My interests range from software development and cyber-physical systems to machine learning and artificial intelligence that have made it possible for humans to build intelligent devices and accomplish as complex of a task as sending a rover all the way to Mars.

Being someone who finds fulfillment in conducting research, I aspire to work in the research-oriented fields of Computer Science and play my part in contributing to the technological advancements.

In my free time I like to play guitar, travel, hike, or read books with a warm cup of coffee in my hand.
